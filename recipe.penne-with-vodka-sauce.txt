Penne with Vodka Sauce
 
Melt in large skillet over med. heat: 
1.5 T butter
1.5 T olive oil
 
Add:
1 onion, finely chopped
 
Cook, stirring, until softened, about 5 minutes. Add:
2 lg garlic cloves, minced
 
Cook, stirring, until just starting to color, about 1 minute. Stir in:
28 oz. can crushed tomatoes
1/4 c. vodka
 
Simmer for 10 minutes. Stir in:
1/2 c. heavy cream
 
Heat through. Stir in:
12 fresh basil leaves, chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
 
Meanwhile, cook until tender but firm:
1 lb penne
 
Toss:
Cooked penne
Sauce
2 grilled chicken breasts, sliced
