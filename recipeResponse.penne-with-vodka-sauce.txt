
Ingredients:
- 1.5 T butter
- 1.5 T olive oil
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 lg garlic cloves, minced
- 28 oz. can crushed tomatoes
- 1/4 c. vodka
- 1/2 c. heavy cream
- 12 fresh basil leaves, chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 lb penne
- 2 grilled chicken breasts, sliced

Instructions:
1. Melt butter and olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Add onion and cook, stirring, until softened, about 5 minutes.
3. Add garlic and cook, stirring, until just starting to color, about 1 minute.
4. Stir in crushed tomatoes and vodka. Simmer for 10 minutes.
5. Stir in heavy cream and heat through.
6. Stir in basil leaves, salt, and pepper.
7. Meanwhile, cook penne until tender but firm.
8. Toss cooked penne with sauce and sliced grilled chicken breasts.

Additional Notes:
- Preparation Time: 10 minutes
- Cooking Time: 15 minutes
- Total Time: 25 minutes
- Serving Size: 4
